# Serenitas - Scriptum Romanus Feature List

## Features for Unauthenticated Citizens

1. **Initium Celeriter (Quick Start):**

   - Engage in a swift Pomodoro session without the need for formalities.

2. **Studium Backgrounds:**

   - Immerse yourself in thematic backgrounds befitting your chosen profile.

3. **Chronica Fortuita (Random Facts Feed):**

   - Peruse a continuous stream of serendipitous facts, curated to delight your intellectual curiosity.

4. **Sententiae Inspiratae (Inspirational Quotes):**

   - Access a treasury of motivational quotes echoing the wisdom of ancient Rome.

5. **Quiz Ludus (Trivia Boosts):**

   - Partake in sporadic quizzes during recess to stimulate your mentis (mind).

6. **Tempus Mechanismus (Customizable Pomodoro Timer):**

   - Tailor the Pomodoro timer settings to suit your work rhythm and preferences.

7. **Musica Concentrare (Background Music):**

   - Revel in a curated musical accompaniment, enhancing your concentration and invoking the spirits of Rome.

8. **Senatus Charta Honoris (Pomodoro Leaderboard):**

   - Compete anonymously with fellow citizens on the global Pomodoro leaderboard.

9. **Eloquentia Progressus (Shareable Achievements):**

   - Showcase your achievements and progress in the app, sharing the glory with the Roman populace.

10. **Offline Harmonia (Offline Background Music):**
    - Enjoy the harmonious melodies even when disconnected from the imperial network.

## Features for Authenticated Senators

11. **Civis Profilum (Personalized Profiles):**

    - Craft your unique senatorial profile, chronicling your triumphs and pursuits.

12. **Arma Personalis (Custom Backgrounds):**

    - Display your heraldic symbols with custom backgrounds, asserting your individuality.

13. **Symphonia Propria (Playlist Customization):**

    - Curate your personal symphony by selecting music from your distinguished collection.

14. **Transitus Inter Themas (Cross-Theme Exploration):**

    - Traverse effortlessly between diverse themes, embracing the intellectual richness each offers.

15. **Consilium Fortitudinis (Advanced Pomodoro Statistics):**

    - Delve into sophisticated statistics, a testament to your dominion over time.

16. **Collaborativa Symphoniae (Collaborative Playlists):**

    - Join hands with other esteemed senators to create harmonious playlists.

17. **Tempus Proventus (Extended Pomodoro Sessions):**

    - Unlock the potential for more extended and profound Pomodoro sessions.

18. **Integratio Muniendi (Task Integration):**

    - Integrate your scrolls and tomes seamlessly for a consolidated work environment.

19. **Fora Communitatis (Community Forums):**

    - Engage in intellectual discourse with fellow senators, sharing insights and strategies.

20. **Sync Multi-Device (Multi-Device Sync):**

    - Synchronize your scrolls and profiles across various devices in true Roman efficiency.

21. **Consilium Trivia (Customizable Trivia Preferences):**

    - Tailor the difficulty and frequency of trivia to match your intellectual prowess.

22. **Imperium Absque Annoyance (Ad-Free Experience):**

    - Relish in an ad-free experience as a mark of distinction for your senatorial status.

23. **Consilium Thematum (Theme Recommendations):**

    - Receive sagacious recommendations tailored to your intellectual preferences.

24. **Charta Honoris Cotidianus (Daily Challenges Leaderboard):**

    - Compete daily for accolades and chart your course to senatorial glory.

25. **Symphoniae Insight (Background Music Insights):**
    - Gain insights into the influence of music on your intellectual focus and productivity.

## Stretching API Integration

- Embrace a regimen of ancient Roman stretching exercises to invigorate the body and mind.
